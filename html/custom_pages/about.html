About Us

A dedicated career portal, mainly to streamline the internship & fresh graduate pipeline, and provide the right talents with quality to our business partners for the growth of their companies. Xremo, is your long-term partner in talent solutions and also an emerging, preferred career source provider in Malaysia and Indonesia.

How We Started

Xremo is started from a simple idea: to assist and support ICT professionals to express their talents confidently with their own personal styles. We also want to provide a platform with technologies to employers in searching for the right staffs for their companies. We have grown rapidly over the past year, impacting more talents and professionals with great position-interests matching and career opportunities.

Our Mission

Providing innovative HR solutions to bring out cooperation, satisfaction and mutual growth opportunities for both jobseekers and employers.

What we do

Xremo empowers jobseekers and employers in pitching and exploring themselves with convenient Video Resume and Digital Resume to enhance the candidate-job matching process. It boosts the relevancy and generates sensational values to both jobseekers and employers.
