Our Service

Xremo offer you a platform where you can find a suitable career and a pool of talent candidates for your company.

Job Posting

Xremo's Job Advertisements consist of a single unique indexable URL with the following sections:-

  • Job Title
  • Position Type
  • Required Experience
  • Description
  • What You'll Get
  • Location
  • Required Education
  • Job Status
  • Responsibilities
  • Company Pitching Video
  • Job Type
  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Salary
  • Nice To Have

Resume Search

The Resume Search Engine enables registered employers to search for candidate's Digital resume. Digital resume may or may not include Video pitches by the candidate.

Sponsored Job AD

The Sponsored Job Ad enables an attached Job Advertisement to be featured prominently at the top of the search results and the matching agent. This is a value added service.

Employer's Banner

The Employer's Banners are image content that an employer may wish to feature on the home page. It can include, but is not limited to, banners, logos or photos of the working environment and people.

Sponsored Employers

The Sponsored Employers section features the brand logos of employers prominently. This is an extra service that may be included as value added services with Advertisement packages.
